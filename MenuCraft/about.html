Our Beginning

Born from a grandmother's kitchen.

MenuCraft began not in a commercial kitchen, but in a small home in Mysuru, where our founder Annapoorna grew up watching her grandmother cook elaborate feasts for every family occasion — weddings, festivals, naming ceremonies.

The magic wasn't just in the recipes — it was in the care. Every dish was made with intention, every combo thoughtfully paired. That philosophy became the soul of MenuCraft.

In 2014, we took that spirit and turned it into a catering service — bringing the warmth of home-cooked South Indian tradition to events across Karnataka.

Our Philosophy

Food that brings people together.

We believe that a great meal does more than feed the body — it creates the atmosphere for real connection. When people eat well together, something special happens.

That's why we don't just take orders — we listen to your vision, understand your guests, and design a meal experience that fits your event perfectly.

Every combo on our menu is a complete story — from the first bite of a crisp vadai to the last spoonful of payasam, every dish is chosen to complement the next.
